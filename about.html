About Us

My Brother’s Keeper-Philippines is a transformational movement dedicated to promoting integrity, servant leadership, and community transformation.

Our Story

As a working arm of GoDucate and an accredited partner of the Philippine National Police (PNP Accreditation No. 2023-09), we work alongside leaders in every sector to inspire change from within.

Through programs such as the Internal Cleansing Program and the Squad Weekly Interactive Meeting (SWIM), we help leaders uphold ethical values, foster unity, and create sustainable change in their communities.

What began as a small initiative has grown into a nationwide movement, touching the lives of thousands of leaders and their communities.

Our Mission

To raise leaders of integrity and servant hearts who will positively impact every sector of society.

Our Vision

A nation led by righteous leaders who serve with humility, justice, and love for people.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We lead by example, upholding honesty and ethical behavior in all we do.

Servant Leadership

We put people first and serve with compassion, humility, and selflessness.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in leadership and community service.
