MBK Honored at 30th PCR Month Culmination Ceremony

My Brotherâ€™s Keeper (MBK) was recognized during the 30th Police Community Relations (PCR) Month Culmination Ceremony, led by NAPOLCOM Commissioner Atty. Ricardo P. Bernabe III on July 31, 2025.

MBK received the Plaque of Merit for its vital role in the moral and spiritual development of PNP personnel, especially through the 21st Century Leadership Training and ongoing spiritual guidance for PRO CALABARZON.

Awardees included PCOL Elmar Sillador (Ret.), Rev. Leo Decinal, and other MBK leaders.

Atty. Bernabe emphasized the values of malasakit, paninindigan, and pananagutan in building a Bagong Pilipinas, while PBGEN Jack L. Wanky thanked all partners for their support. The event also featured a recap of activities like medical missions and outreach programs.
