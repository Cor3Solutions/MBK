MBK Honored at 30th PCR Month Culmination Ceremony

My Brother’s Keeper (MBK) was recognized during the 30th Police Community Relations (PCR) Month Culmination Ceremony, led by NAPOLCOM Commissioner Atty. Ricardo P. Bernabe III on July 31, 2025.

MBK received the Plaque of Merit for its vital role in the moral and spiritual development of PNP personnel, especially through the 21st Century Leadership Training and ongoing spiritual guidance for PRO CALABARZON.

Awardees included PCOL Elmar Sillador (Ret.), Rev. Leo Decinal, and other MBK leaders.

Atty. Bernabe emphasized the values of malasakit, paninindigan, and pananagutan in building a Bagong Pilipinas, while PBGEN Jack L. Wanky thanked all partners for their support. The event also featured a recap of activities like medical missions and outreach programs.

PNP CALABARZON Personnel Gather for DPPAR and Inspirational Session at Camp Vicente Lim

Personnel of PRO CALABARZON assembled at Camp BGen Vicente P. Lim for the Daily PNP Personnel Accounting Report (DPPAR), organized by the RIDMD to ensure accountability and share regional updates.

Rev. Leo Decinal, National Coordinator of My Brother’s Keeper (MBK), delivered an inspirational message introducing the acronym E-W.A.N.K.Y. encouraging excellence, wholehearted work, integrity, and faith in God.

PCOL Christopher P. Bad-ang also emphasized the importance of discipline, consistency, and maintaining order within camp premises.

MBK Recognized and Expands Support Through Digital SWIM Launch

My Brother’s Keeper (MBK) was honored at the 30th PCR Month Culmination Ceremony with a Plaque of Merit for its spiritual and moral support to PNP personnel, especially in PRO CALABARZON.

MBK also partnered with PRO1 to launch the Digitalized SWIM (Squad Weekly Interactive Meeting) at Camp Florendo in La Union, aiming to expand moral guidance through accessible online content.

The initiative, supported by PMGEN Mao Aplasca (Ret.), PCOL Elmar Sillador (Ret.), and Rev. Leo Decinal, was positively received and reinforces character, integrity, and discipline within the police force.

Digitalized SWIM Launched in PRO3 to Boost Moral Development

Police Regional Office 3, led by PBGEN Ponce Rogelio I. Peñones, Jr., launched the Digitalized SWIM (Squad Weekly Interactive Meeting) at Camp Captain Julian Olivas in Pampanga to accelerate the rollout of the program.

In partnership with MBK leaders PMGEN Mao Aplasca (Ret.), PCOL Elmar Sillador (Ret.), and Rev. Leo Decinal, the initiative aims to provide consistent, values-based guidance to PNP personnel in PRO3 through digital tools.

The event gathered key police leaders and received positive feedback for its potential to strengthen integrity, accountability, and personal growth within the force.

My Brother’s Keeper-Philippines is a transformational movement for righteous leadership in all sectors. We are a proud partner of the Philippine National Police in building integrity and leadership.
