What We Do

Empowering Leaders. Transforming Communities.

My Brother’s Keeper-Philippines is a transformational movement for righteous leadership. In partnership with GoDucate and the Philippine National Police, we actively engage in programs that develop integrity, servant leadership, and accountability in all sectors of society.

Leadership Training

We equip leaders in communities and organizations with values-based leadership skills through workshops, seminars, and mentorship.

Learn More

PNP Internal Cleansing Program

As an accredited partner of the PNP, we support the Internal Cleansing Program through the Squad Weekly Interactive Meeting (SWIM).

Learn More

Values Formation

We conduct values formation sessions that instill integrity, discipline, and service for personal and community transformation.

Learn More

Community Outreach

We partner with local communities to provide programs, outreach initiatives, and holistic support for sustainable impact.
