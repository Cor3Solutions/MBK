Internal Cleansing Program

As an accredited partner of the Philippine National Police (PNP Accreditation No. 2023-09), MBK works closely with the PNP to restore integrity, discipline, and service excellence among its members.

  • Implementation of the Internal Cleansing Program.
  • Leadership coaching and mentoring for officers.
  • Workshops focused on ethics, accountability, and discipline.
  • Spiritual guidance to strengthen moral values.

SWIM (Squad Weekly Interactive Meeting)

SWIM is a values-based weekly small group meeting for police squads designed to encourage fellowship, accountability, and personal growth.

  • Conducted in small squad settings for deeper discussions.
  • Focus on moral values, integrity, and servant leadership.
  • Promotes camaraderie and trust among officers.
  • Helps officers align their personal values with public service duties.

Community Outreach

We partner with local communities to provide outreach programs that address real needs while promoting unity and service.

  • Feeding programs and relief distribution.
  • Educational workshops and youth leadership training.
  • Collaboration with barangays and local organizations.
  • Holistic support for long-term community development.

Special Events & Trainings

MBK organizes conferences, leadership summits, and specialized training sessions to empower leaders and communities for greater impact.

  • Annual leadership summits and networking events.
  • Skills development workshops for community leaders.
  • Training on servant leadership and ethical governance.
  • Collaborations with experts and partner organizations.
